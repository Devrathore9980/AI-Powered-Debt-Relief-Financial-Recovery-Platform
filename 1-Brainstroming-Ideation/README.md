# Brainstorming & Ideation

## Problem Statement
Borrowers struggling with debt often don't know how to negotiate settlements with lenders, 
track their financial health, or generate professional negotiation letters on their own.

## Idea
Build an AI-powered platform (FinRelief AI) that:
- Analyzes a borrower's loan and financial profile
- Predicts a fair settlement percentage using rule-based logic
- Uses Google Gemini AI to generate negotiation strategies and letters
- Tracks financial health and debt history over time

## Target Users
Borrowers with multiple loans (personal loans, credit cards, EMIs) who want a 
data-driven, AI-assisted way to negotiate with lenders.
